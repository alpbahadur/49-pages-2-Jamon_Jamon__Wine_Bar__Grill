Our Story

#piglove

Born from a passion for exceptional cuisine and inspired by the iconic Spanish film "Jamón Jamón," Jamon Jamon brings together the warmth of Mediterranean hospitality with the bold flavors of Italian and Spanish cuisine.

Founded by Argentinian restaurateur Sergio Bevilacqua, who brings over three decades of gastronomic expertise, our restaurant is a tribute to family gatherings, passionate conversations, and the art of sharing incredible food.

Under the culinary leadership of Chef Manuel Naula, we craft each dish with meticulous attention to detail, celebrating the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques.
